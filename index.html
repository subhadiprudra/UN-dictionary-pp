UN Translator Privacy Policy

This privacy policy describes how UN Translator, created by Tithi Pal, collects, uses, and shares your personal information.

Information We Collect

UN Translator does not collect any personal information directly from you.

However, we do use third-party services that may collect certain information, which is detailed below:

  • **Microphone permission:** This permission is required for speech recognition, a core feature of the app. No audio data is stored or transmitted.
  • **Accessibility permission:** This permission is used to translate text from other apps. No data is accessed or transmitted without your explicit consent.
  • **Overlay permission:** This permission is used to display translated text on top of other apps. No data is accessed or transmitted.

Third-party services used by UN Translator and their privacy policies:

  • Google Play Services: https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • Google Analytics for Firebase: https://firebase.google.com/terms/analytics
  • Firebase Crashlytics: https://firebase.google.com/terms/crashlytics
  • Appodeal: https://appodeal.com/privacy-policy/
  • One Signal: https://onesignal.com/privacy_policy
  • AppLovin: https://www.applovin.com/privacy-policy/

Use of Information

The information collected by third-party services is used for the following purposes:

  • **To improve the app's performance and stability (Firebase Crashlytics).
  • **To understand how users interact with the app (Google Analytics for Firebase).
  • **To provide targeted advertising (Appodeal, One Signal, AppLovin, while respecting privacy settings and user consent).

Sharing of Information

We do not share any personal information with third-party companies or individuals.

Data Retention

We do not store any personal information.

Security

We take appropriate security measures to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction of any data you provide, although we cannot guarantee its absolute security.
